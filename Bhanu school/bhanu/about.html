About us

Center of Excellence

New Capital College is an English medium college, conceived and established by a group of scholars, experts and educationists aiming to meet the needs of intelligent students with the motto of "CENTRE OF EXCELLENCE" in 2057 B.S.

Members of New Capital College management team possess a wide range of experience and vision suitable for the 21st century.

With a large number of students attending classes every day and with sufficiently qualified and experienced teachers, NCC has made rapid strides of progress in the field of education and has emerged as one of the best and popular colleges in Chitwan for higher studies. It has Science, Management and Education stream under NEB and BBS affiliated to TU.
